WHO WE ARE

Pioneering Biopharmaceutical Excellence

Combining decades of scientific expertise with cutting-edge AI technology to develop next-generation therapeutics

American Biosources Inc. stands at the forefront of biopharmaceutical innovation, leveraging advanced artificial intelligence to revolutionize drug discovery and development. As a clinical-stage company, we focus on developing universal seasonal and pandemic influenza vaccines alongside breakthrough therapeutic proteins and biosimilars.

Our multidisciplinary team combines deep scientific expertise with state-of-the-art computational biology, machine learning, and bioprocess engineering to accelerate the development of life-saving treatments. We are committed to addressing the most pressing healthcare challenges of our time through innovative research and strategic partnerships.

With a strong foundation in regulatory excellence and quality assurance, we ensure that every product we develop meets the highest standards of safety and efficacy, bringing hope to patients worldwide.
