About Us

Who We Are

AMBIOINC was founded with a clear vision to drive innovation in the biopharmaceutical industry and contribute meaningful solutions to global healthcare challenges.

We focus on addressing critical unmet medical needs by leveraging our expertise in biologics, research, and advanced technologies to improve patient outcomes and make a lasting impact on lives.

Our Mission

Improving Lives By Quality, Affordability, Availability & Accessibility. To be amongst the leading biopharma companies in the world by extending our presence globally through quality and cost-effective biologics, biotherapeutics and biosimilars to compassionately address human and animal suffering.

Our Vision

Time to Market Quality Products for unmet Medical Needs. By leveraging extensive biotech expertise, we envision creating innovative biologics, speciality pharmaceuticals and biosimilars businesses globally.

Our Strategy

Our strategy focuses on time-to-market solutions for affordability, accessibility, and availability for equitable distribution of COVID-19 and other infectious disease vaccines, biological drugs, gene therapy, and therapeutic proteins (innovator & biosimilar) for unmet medical needs.

About ABSNovacell Biopharmaceuticals Limited

ABSNovacell Biopharmaceuticals Limited is a spun off from American Biosources Inc., NJ, USA. ABSINC was originally established in year 2001, having R & D labs and developmental laboratories in USA. Technology licenses and transfers for commercialization to India and rest of the world.

The company was originally founded by two world-renowned scientists with over 50 years of combined experience, along with global biopharma executives, on a strong foundation of research innovation, novelty and intellectual property generation. We also possess extensive expertise with the USFDA, EMEA, MHRA and WHO.

ABSNovacell Biopharmaceuticals Limited is a Biopharmaceutical & Biotechnology R&D, Process Development and Clinical stage company. We have commercial products in speciality Pharmaceuticals, Nutraceuticals, Diagnostics and Swiss cosmetics.
